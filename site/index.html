What We Do

Full-Service Auto Repair

From oil changes to major engine work, our family handles it all. No upsells, no surprises. Just straight talk from people who know cars.

Oil Change & Lube

Full synthetic, conventional, and high-mileage oil service. Filter swap, fluid top-offs, and a multi-point check every time. We do not rush it and we do not cut corners. Your engine depends on this service more than any other.

  • Full synthetic, conventional, and high-mileage options
  • Oil filter replacement every visit
  • Fluid top-offs included
  • Multi-point visual inspection

Brake Service

Brake pad replacement, rotor resurfacing or replacement, caliper service, and brake fluid flushes. We do not guess. We inspect first and show you what we find before any work begins.

  • Brake pad inspection and replacement
  • Rotor resurfacing or full replacement

Engine Diagnostics & Repair

Check engine light? We run full computer diagnostics and explain what it actually means. No jargon, no pressure. From timing belts to full engine rebuilds, our mechanics have the hands-on experience that only decades in the shop can build.

  • Full OBD-II computer diagnostics
  • Timing belt and chain service
  • Head gasket repair
  • Full engine rebuilds

AC & Heating

  • AC system service and recharge

  • Heater core replacement
  • Cabin air filter service
