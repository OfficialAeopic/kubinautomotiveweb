Our Story

Three Generations. One Family. One Standard.

Since 1978, the Kubin family has served Bryan and the Brazos Valley with the same founding principle: fix what needs fixing, charge a fair price, never oversell.

Founded 1978

Where It Started

The first Kubin to open the doors on South College Avenue did so with a simple belief: people deserve to know exactly what is wrong with their car and exactly what it will cost to fix it. No hidden fees. No unnecessary repairs. No runaround.

That belief became the foundation of a shop that would outlast trends, competition, and decades of change in the automotive industry. Customers who first walked in as young adults brought their children. Their children now bring their own vehicles.

Kolton Kubin carries that standard forward as the third generation of his family to run this shop. The equipment has been updated. The methods have evolved. The honesty has never changed.

Our History

48 Years in the Brazos Valley

From a single-bay shop to a full-service repair center, the Kubin name has been synonymous with honest automotive care in Bryan, TX for nearly five decades.

1978

Kubin Automotive Service opens on South College Avenue in Bryan, TX. The founding generation establishes the no-upsell standard that defines the shop to this day.

1990s

The second generation joins the family trade. The shop expands its service capabilities and builds a loyal base of repeat customers across Bryan and College Station.

2000s

Computer diagnostics and modern systems become standard. Kubin Automotive invests in updated equipment while maintaining the same family-first service model.

2010s

Fleet service expands as local businesses in the Brazos Valley trust Kubin Automotive for priority commercial vehicle maintenance and repair.

Today

Kolton Kubin leads the third generation while also completing his degree at Texas A&M. A local and an Aggie, he carries forward the family tradition of honest, no-upsell service that has defined this shop since 1978.
