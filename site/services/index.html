Everything Your Vehicle Needs

Oil Change & Lube

Your engine's lifeblood. We do not rush oil changes and we do not cut corners. Every visit includes a filter swap, fluid top-offs, and a multi-point visual check so nothing sneaks up on you between services.

  • Full synthetic, conventional, and high-mileage options
  • Oil filter replacement every visit
  • All fluid levels checked and topped off
  • Multi-point visual inspection included
  • Tire pressure check
  • Wiper blade and light inspection

Brake Service

Brakes are the single most important safety system on your vehicle. We inspect first and show you exactly what we find before any work begins. No guessing, no pressure, no unnecessary parts.

  • Brake pad inspection and replacement
  • Rotor resurfacing or full replacement
  • Caliper service and hardware
  • Drum brake shoes and hardware
  • Brake fluid flush and refill
  • ABS and brake warning light diagnostics

Engine Diagnostics & Repair

Check engine light? We run full computer diagnostics and explain what it actually means in plain English. No jargon, no pressure. From timing belts to major engine work, our mechanics have built this expertise over decades in the shop.

  • Full OBD-II computer diagnostics
  • Check engine light diagnosis
  • Timing belt and timing chain service
  • Head gasket repair and replacement
  • Cylinder head and valve train work
  • Full engine rebuilds

AC & Heating

Texas heat is no joke. We service and recharge AC systems, diagnose compressor and condenser issues, and repair heater cores so you are comfortable year-round. Do not wait until July to find out your AC is down.

  • AC system service and recharge (R-134a and R-1234yf)
  • Compressor diagnosis and repair
  • Condenser and evaporator service
  • Heater core flush and replacement
  • Blower motor and fan service
  • Cabin air filter replacement

Transmission Service

Transmission problems caught early are transmission problems fixed cheaply. We help you stay ahead of issues through regular fluid and filter service, and we handle diagnostic and repair work when something does go wrong.

  • Automatic and manual transmission service
  • Transmission fluid flush and replacement
  • Filter changes for automatic transmissions
  • Slipping, hard-shift, and no-start diagnostics
  • Transmission rebuild referrals when needed
  • Transfer case and differential service

Suspension & Steering

If your vehicle pulls, bounces, wanders, or shakes, your suspension or steering is telling you something. We diagnose and repair the full system so your ride handles safely and comfortably on every road.

  • Shock and strut inspection and replacement
  • Tie rod ends and inner tie rods
  • Ball joint replacement
  • Wheel bearing service
  • Alignment inspection
  • Power steering fluid and rack service
Learn More

Electrical & Battery

Modern vehicles are computers on wheels. We are fluent in both the mechanical and the digital. From a dead battery to complex wiring faults, we trace the problem and fix it correctly the first time.

  • Battery testing and replacement
  • Alternator and charging system repair
  • Starter motor replacement
  • Wiring and fuse diagnostics
  • Lighting and accessory circuits
  • Instrument cluster and warning light diagnosis

Preventive Maintenance

The cheapest repair is the one you never need. We help you stay on your manufacturer's maintenance schedule so small wear items never become big failures on the side of the road.

  • Full tune-up service
  • Spark plug and ignition wire replacement

  • Radiator and coolant hose service
